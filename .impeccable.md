## Design Context

### Users
AI agent builders and MCP users (Cursor / Claude) who want Israeli supermarket prices, promotions, and basket optimization inside their agent workflow. They arrive to connect quickly, not to browse docs.

### Brand Personality
Concrete, local, useful. Grocery-real Israel energy: warm market stall, not Silicon Valley chrome. Confident without hype.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Single-page Hebrew RTL marketing site, light-only warm limestone paper
- Accent: paprika / shuk-red; support: muted olive; ink: olive-charcoal
- Typography: Rubik or Heebo + mono for technical LTR strings (no Inter)
- Soft ~12px radius system; sparse cards; real market imagery
- Anti-references: purple AI glow, neon dark mode, fake terminal heroes, generic three equal feature cards

### Design Principles
1. One conversion intent: התחבר (Cursor deep-link + on-page copy fallback).
2. Prove locality with real Israeli places, chains, and basket language.
3. Keep components small: shadcn primitives + shared utilities + thin marketing sections.
4. Hero is brand + one claim + CTA + full-bleed visual; nothing else.
5. Prefer concrete Hebrew over marketing fluff.
